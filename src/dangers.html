There are many risks associated with the development and progression of Artificial Intelligence.

Dangers

Danger of Programming

There are many ways that artificial intelligence can become a threat, however, the easiest way for AI to become a danger is the programming. Be it malicious intent from the programmmer or misinterpretation of the task, AI can become a severe threat to humanity. For instance, AI may be created to monitor and keep weapons systems in check, however, if there were to be fault in the program there could be potentially devatating effects.

Prevention

Asimov's Three Laws of Robotics

While artificial intelligence can be a great threat to humanity if improperly programmed and utilized, they can also be a great asset towards the development of future technology. As such artificial intelligence needs to be properly created and controlled. One such method is the utilization of Asimov’s Three Laws of Robotics.

  • A robot may not injure a human being or, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm.
  • A robot must obey the orders given to it by human beings except where such orders would conflict with the First Law.
  • A robot must protect its own existence as long as such protection does not conflict with the First or Second Laws.
