Prevention

Asimov's Three Laws of Robotics

While artificial intelligence can be a great threat to humanity if improperly programmed and utilized, they can also be a great asset towards the development of future technology. As such artificial intelligence needs to be properly created and controlled. One such method is the utilization of Asimovâ€™s Three Laws of Robotics.

  • A robot may not injure a human being or, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm.
  • A robot must obey the orders given to it by human beings except where such orders would conflict with the First Law.
  • A robot must protect its own existence as long as such protection does not conflict with the First or Second Laws.
