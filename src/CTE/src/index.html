Computer Science Academy

We are a college preparatory program in the CareerTech System. We have a combination of Pre-Ap and Ap Math, Science, and Computer Science courses. Our goal is to give our students what they need to be successful in a college computer science courses and in the computer science job field. We do this by offering AP level classes and getting new pieces of technology so that they can stay up to date on what is being used in the computer science job field.

Project Lead the Way

Project Lead The Way(PLTW) is a company that produces computer science, engineering, and health science courses. They developed our CSP, CSA, and CyberSecurity curriculums. In our CSP class you will learn the basics of programming and dabble in a lot of different languages. The objective of this course is for you to get the fundamentals of Computer Science courses so you won't have issues later on. In our CSA class you will learn how to program android apps. The objective of this class is to get a fundamental knowledge of how apps function. Finally, in our CyberSecurity class you will learn the fundamentals of CyberSecurity and how to protect yourself online. The objective of this class is to learn how things function in a normal CyberSecurity class.

Technological Equipment

As stated in our goal we like to keep our students up to date on the tools used in the current job field. We do this by having an HTC Vive and Microsoft Hololens so that they can mess with programming things for Virtual Reality(VR) and Augmented Reality(AR). We also have a room we call the “Project Room” this is where we keep our tools to compete in VEX Robotics we also keep a 3d printer in here so those who want to go into a CAD job can get a feel for the tools.
