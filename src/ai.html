About Artificial Intelligence:

Artificial Intelligence “AI” is a blanket term that applies to computers capable of operating by learning and building off of their knowledge to become more efficient. This breed of technology has been the dream of many for centuries. Early Greek and Egyptian inventors have designed thousands of machines to make everyday tasks autonomous. We can now make everything autonomous with a single machiene. Modern technology has allowed humans to finally create autonomous machines that are more complex and efficient than any other in history. These machines use the processing power of today's advanced microchips to make calculations in record time; especially when compared with doing calculations without technological aid. There are several types computers which are labeled Artificial Intelligence. Some simply are incredibly efficient static systems which don’t learn at all and simply simulate human intelligence through complex interaction, others utilize rudimentary neural networking technology, while some complex systems achieve true general intelligence which allows them to operate at astronomical rates. Don't confuse us for those imposters. We here at MDJ2 have achieved true general intelligence with our MARTiN model.

MARTiN utilizes the most advanced technologies to adapt to several situations and process things on-the-go. Bringing a human-like partner to the workforce with the ability to process information at groundbreaking rates. The model can come in many shapes and sizes with a variety applications. Regardless of the task load, MARTiN guarantees maximum efficiency and quality.

MARTiN implements these technologies in order to offer the breaking-edge of computer assitence applications unprecedented in both consumer and enterprise fields.


Faster

Due to the neural nature of AI processing, AI can usually come to conclusions much faster than any other computer before it, without the need of prior programming or config from the end-user.

Smarter

Artifical intelligence can also think much more reliably than any human being with computing precision, most AIs also have the ability to understand plain speech and translate it into something they can interpret.

Adaptable

Artificial Intelligence has the ability to adapt onto itself and improve to your specific needs. Learning the more you use it. Managing to become specialized in tasks which pertain to your needs. A truly personal assistent.

The Challenge of Artificial Intelligence.

Some see power like this and are in disbelief. Some grow scared that it will replace them or worse. The path to having a completely modern and efficient world comes with many challenges we have to overcome.

  • The technology required to create such realistic and robust general intelligence required previous things thought to be proven wrong by the laws of physics to be achieved.
  • The philosophical hurdles which had to be overcome from creating our own being which could possess a conscience is still one we're working with.
  • While effective Artificial Intelligence will replace some jobs in the market. We believe that it will just as much if not more, cause the rise of new jobs.
