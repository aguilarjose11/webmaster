What is Computer Science Academy? (CSA)

The computer science academy is a meticulous program with only Pre-AP and AP courses that focus on the development of computer science skills and the study of the different areas devoting principally to prepare its students for college-level computer science curriculums.

The computer science academy, being only-of-its-kind program in the state, and the first computer science program of this kind in Oklahoma, in the computer science academy the science, math, and computer science curriculums are combined into one, this results in a study of math and science focused for use in computer science problems

Our actual Logo was designed by the 2016-2017 freshman CSA Student Grace Hopper, in her design she displays the Francis Tuttle Purple duck, the Francis Tuttle Mascot, and arranges the name of our Program to create it.

The computer science academy offers classes in Computer Science, Math, and Sciences.

Computer Science.

in the computer science area the students have options for classes that prepare them for real life computer science applications.

  • AP Computer Science Principles (APCSPLTW).
  • Computer Science and Software Engineering.
  • Computer Science Applications.
  • Computer Science Artificial Intelligence.
  • Computer Science Cybersecurity.
  • Computer Science Capstone: Computational Problem Solving.
  • Java Programming I.
  • C# I and II

Math.

Math in the Computer Science Academy is one of the most important things in the curriculum, the offerings are all Pre-AP or AP only.

  • Trigonometry/Pre-Calculus.
  • Pre AP Algebra II.
  • AP Calculus AB.

Science.

Science in the Computer Science Academy is the final cherry on top of the three pillars of CSA
