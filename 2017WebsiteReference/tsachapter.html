Francis Tuttle TSA, a story of Leadership

In FrancisTuttle we have been working every day, from easy routines to hard projects that showcase our intense work and interest in the world of technology. With a campus program that expects students to do their best is, the best way to learn coding.
Francistuttle is a multiprogram campus that has 2 periods:

  • First period from 8:00 - 11:00
  • Second period from 12:30 - 3:30

It's during this day that we work on our TSA Projects, every wednsday we dedicate an hour to TSA were we get to talk to our teammates and plan ahead the projects we may be working on.
