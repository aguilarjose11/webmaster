Meet Virtual reality...


When people talk about virtual reality, they refer to the computer technologies that use software to generate realistic images, sounds and other sensations replicating the environment also simulating the user's physical presence in the environment.

 These days, humanity has been able to recreate environments inside computers , these environments are now accessible using virtual reality which based on data that is recollected with virtual reality devices shows us the different aspects of these virtual environments.

PAST


Virtual reality was achieved in some ways using panoramic paintings, stereoscopic photos and simulators in the past like the ones used to simulate flight.

Science fiction also expanded in the field by creating stories and movies that focused on technologies that would let the users feel the experience of the movie as they were in it.

PRESENT


These days virtual reality is used in many fields like: gaming, daily life, sciences, and sports.

Virtual reality has slowly expanded into a modern technology that gives promise of making life easier, better and well prepared.

Virtual reality is still a field under development so we can expect more inventions in time.

Gaming.

Virtual Reality has slowly gained popularity thanks to e-sports and as a novelty. The gaming world has been the one that has seen the most popular grow, as companies spend more time reasearching and creatinag games, people are slowly being able to afford virtural reality. It is expected that by 2020 virtual reality will be a gaming device that will be involved in almost all aspects of gaming.

Studies.

Virtual reality has proven effective in teaching and studying many topics like science, architecture, engineering, math and even world languages. Todays graphics have evolved to a point that virtual reality can emulate real time situations like surgeries, encounters with foreign people, and even a presentation to a group of people.

Daily Life.

One of the most interesting and innovative aspects that virtual reality has achieved is the influence it has in real life activities and allowing these activities to be accomplished faster and more effectively; these days talking to people has taken a new level thanks to virtual reality, now it is possible to emulate a person to person conversations giving electronic communications a new factor: Physical language inclusion.

Pros.

Thanks to Virtual Reality, many achievements has been obtained, examples of these are:

  • Modern and more efficient ways create simulations
  • Better preparation for professional careers that need field experience.
  • New and more complex communication systems.
  • Improvements in designs using reality testing.

Cons.

as anything good in life has drawbacks:

  • Social consequences for its addictive quality.
  • Virtual reality equipment really expensive.
  • Video quality of system.
  • Lack of laws regulating virtual reality.

Why VR in the first place?

Virtual Reality can be a tool that can create new worlds or destroy existing ones. virtual reality has the potential to one day create better prepared professionals that will excel in their professions, a example of this will be surgery simulation programs, where the realism of these procedures are extremely similar to the ones of every day. Virtual Reality has proven potential in the design, testing and implementation of solutions for different fields of the industrial world as well as the research world, also better graphics and more realistic models have achieved an increase in the functionality, performance and reliability of many different already existing technologies or creating new ones , it's now possible to see and test models inside a virtual environment without risking real life human lives.
